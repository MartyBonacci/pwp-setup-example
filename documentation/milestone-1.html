PWP Milestone 1 and 2 Example

Purpose

What is my purpose for making this website?

Audience

What type of person do I want to use my website?

Goals

What do I want the website to do for me?

Persona

This is a fictional but realistic person to imagine is your ideal user. Make this part detailed enough that it feels like we just had an hour interview with the user to understand their needs, preferences, and behaviors. Consider their goals, challenges, and motivations when crafting this persona.

User Story

As a ____ user, I want to accomplish ____.
